### **Part 1: Technical Breakthroughs & Human Robot Interaction**

- **Overcoming Hardware Limits:** Successfully built a robust, end-to-end architecture operating entirely within a strict 8GB memory limit.
- **Edge-Deployed Multi-Sensor Fusion:** Efficiently integrated natural voice interaction, real-time vision tracking, and strict obstacle safety directly on edge devices.
- **Empathetic Interaction:** Achieved a genuine human-robot interaction where PawAI detects user intent, responds warmly with voice, sways its body, and performs happy steps.

### **Part 2: Future Outlook & The Evolution of Physical AI**

- **Scalable Applications:** Seamlessly extending our foundational platform from home care to advanced medical assistance, disaster rescue, and mobility guide roles.
- **Intelligent Physical Action:** Evolving from perception-based interactions to intelligent physical actions by integrating Vision-Language-Action (VLA) and World Models.
- **Embodied AI Milestones:** Creating a system that can deeply understand environments, predict action outcomes, and plan safer, adaptive behaviors in the real world.
